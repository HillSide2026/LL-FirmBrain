# POL-018 — Codified Distribution Requirement

Policy Statement: As much as possible, all Solutions or Strategies distributed by LL must be codified in ML2 prior to distribution. Codification includes governing playbooks and required downstream operational artifacts.
Authority (Principles referenced): PRN-005, PRN-017, PRN-020, PRN-021
Enforcement expectation: Distribution of non-codified Solutions or Strategies is non-compliant unless an explicit ML1 exception is recorded.
