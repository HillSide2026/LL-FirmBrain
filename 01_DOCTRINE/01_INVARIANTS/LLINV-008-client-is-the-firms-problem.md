# LLINV-008 — Client Is the Firm's Problem

**Invariant ID:** LLINV-008
**Status:** APPROVED
**Authority:** ML1

---

## 1. Purpose

Define the institutional character of the client relationship. The client relationship runs to Levine Law, not to ML1 personally.

---

## 2. The Rule

The client is the firm's problem. Not ML1's personal problem.

---

## 3. Invariants

1. The client relationship is with Levine Law as an institution.
2. Client obligations are professional and scoped: they are defined by the engagement, the retainer, and the firm's professional duties. They are not personal and unlimited.
3. The client's problem belongs to the firm within the scope of the engagement. Outside that scope, it does not belong to the firm, and it does not belong to ML1.
