# PRN-039 — Governed Work Granularity

Title: Governed Work Granularity

Statement:
Governance labels should preserve actual work granularity. Portfolios, programs,
projects, milestones, and tasks should remain structurally distinct rather than
being collapsed into one another for convenience.

Rationale:
Granularity fidelity keeps registers, stage gates, plans, and reporting
legible.
