# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-041
Project Path: LLP-046/LLP-041

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: the parked tier may become a default holding state rather than a deliberate service posture.
2. Scope risk: matters may be parked without clear entry and exit criteria.
3. Schedule risk: parked matters may accumulate unresolved follow-up delays.
4. Budget risk: monitoring dormant matters may consume more administrative time than planned.
5. Financial risk: parked matters may tie up capacity without commensurate return.

## Key Assumptions
- The parked tier will be used intentionally rather than as a catch-all category.
- Entry and exit criteria can be documented clearly enough to guide application.
- Parked matters will still receive enough review to avoid silent stagnation.
- The monitoring burden will remain proportionate to the value of maintaining the tier.
- Capacity costs from parked matters can be tolerated or reduced through policy.
