# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-040
Project Path: 09_SERVICE_MANAGEMENT/STANDARD

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: the standard-tier definition may fail to differentiate clearly from other service tiers.
2. Scope risk: standard-tier boundaries may expand inconsistently across matters.
3. Schedule risk: delivery commitments may slip if standard-tier expectations remain unclear.
4. Budget risk: standard-tier support effort may exceed baseline operating assumptions.
5. Financial risk: standard-tier economics may underperform once support costs are measured.

## Key Assumptions
- The standard tier can be described clearly enough to avoid overlap with adjacent tiers.
- Matter teams can apply standard-tier rules consistently.
- Expected service cadence can be delivered within available capacity.
- Support load will remain within planned administrative assumptions.
- Pricing and margin assumptions for the tier will remain acceptable.
