# Problem Statement

Project ID: LLP-003
Project Path: 05_MATTER_DOCKETING/LLP-003

## Current Problem
- The matter-docketing program needs a bounded weekly reporting surface, but LLP-003 did not yet have a canonical initiation packet describing what the report is, what it reads from, and what decisions it does not make.

## Impact
- Without a governed packet, weekly reporting can drift in content, miss key escalations, or blur the line between reporting and source-of-truth status management.

## Why Now
- Adjacent docketing packets already exist, and the weekly report should be anchored before further automation or reporting conventions accumulate.
