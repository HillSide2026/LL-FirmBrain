# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-027
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-014/LLP-027

## Top Risks

1. Channel blind spots: inquiries arriving via phone or referral are not
   logged and fall outside the system, creating an unmeasured inquiry stream
   alongside the governed one.
2. Triage ambiguity: unclear practice area classification criteria result in
   inquiries being misrouted or held for ML1 review when they should route
   automatically.
3. SLA failure: without a defined and enforced response time, leads go cold
   before initial contact is made.
4. Handoff failure: inquiries qualified for consultation do not reach LLP-028
   reliably — handoff protocol is undefined or not triggered.
5. Volume capacity: inquiry volume exceeds operational capacity without a
   priority or capacity management layer, degrading response quality across
   the board.

## Key Assumptions

- LLP-026 (Lead Capture) will deliver web form submissions in a format that
  allows automated logging into this system
- LLP-028 (Consults) will define what it needs from an incoming handoff before
  the handoff protocol is finalized in Planning
- ML1 will approve SLA targets and triage criteria before they are implemented
