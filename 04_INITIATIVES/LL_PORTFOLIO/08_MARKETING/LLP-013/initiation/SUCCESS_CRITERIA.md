# Success Criteria

Project ID: LLP-013
Project Path: 08_MARKETING/LLP-013

The project will be successful if:

1. Funnel 03 scope and authority boundaries are explicit and ML1-approved.
2. The canonical entry-offer map is explicit and internally consistent:
   - `MSB Registration Mandate`
   - `Suspicious Transaction Triage & STR Filing`
   - `MSB Health Check`
3. The canonical core-offer map is explicit and internally consistent:
   - `Suspicious Transaction Reporting Triage & Playbook`
   - `MSB Effectiveness Review`
   - `RPAA Reporting`
   - `Ongoing AML Counsel Retainer`
4. Entry-offer orchestration is documented and operationally coherent.
5. QA and distribution controls are defined for regulated-domain messaging.
6. Conversion and revenue-attribution tracking requirements are established.
7. Market signal reporting produces clear refinement candidates for ML1 review.
8. A networking-based business development lane is documented, bounded, and attributable inside Funnel 03.
