# Problem Statement

Project ID: LLP-038

## Problem

Levine Law currently delivers legal services without a standardized, templated
workflow. Each matter is handled from first principles. This creates three
compounding risks:

1. **Delegation is not credible.** Without defined stages, hour caps, and
   checklists, there is no basis on which to supervise or QA an associate's work.

2. **Unit economics cannot be measured.** Without per-service hour budgets,
   realization rates, yield, and capacity models are estimates at best.

3. **Assetization is blocked.** A firm whose delivery depends entirely on
   ML1 improvisation cannot be packaged, valued, or transferred.

## Why Now

LLP-033 (Associate Lawyer) and LLP-039 (Fintech Retainer) both require the
Spine before their planning can be completed. The 2027 associate hire decision
requires demonstrably repeatable work. Deferring the Spine defers the entire
leverage and scale strategy.
