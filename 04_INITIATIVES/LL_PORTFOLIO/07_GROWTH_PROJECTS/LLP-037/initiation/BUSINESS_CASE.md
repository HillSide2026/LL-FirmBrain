# Business Case

Project ID: LLP-037
Stage: Planning

## Rationale

Levine Law needs an authoritative system design to coordinate the gap between
matter creation, legal work execution, document generation, client delivery,
and future internal tooling. Without a governed design, adjacent builds can
optimize locally while creating integration debt and delivery friction.

## Expected Value

- Clarifies the Legal Work System layer between matter creation and document delivery.
- Reduces duplicated tooling decisions across LL-WrkEngine, ll-corporate, and related systems.
- Supports delivery capacity by making future system work sequenced and governed.

## Approval Boundary

This is a draft business case. ML1 remains the approval authority for system
architecture, project advancement, and build execution.
