# Success Criteria

The project will be successful if:

1. A defined Associate scope of authority is documented.
2. Supervision doctrine is formalized and defensible.
3. Review standards are measurable and enforceable.
4. Financial modeling confirms sustainable margin structure.
5. ML1 formally approves hiring and activation.
