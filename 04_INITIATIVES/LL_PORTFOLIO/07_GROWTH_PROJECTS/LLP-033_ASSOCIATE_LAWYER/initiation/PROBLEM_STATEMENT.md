# Problem Statement

Levine Law’s current structure concentrates legal authority and execution in ML1.

This creates:
- Throughput limitations
- Revenue ceiling constraints
- Elevated key-person risk
- Limited scalability of recurring services
- Inability to properly supervise delegated roles at scale

A structured Associate Lawyer role is required to support multi-tier service delivery.
