# Project Charter

Project ID: LLP-038
Project Name: Delivery Spine
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/07_GROWTH_PROJECTS/LLP-038
Project Type: Strategic Project
Stage: Planning

## 1. Purpose

Define and build the operational structure through which Levine Law delivers
legal services: a standardized, templated workflow from intake through output
and close, scoped in hours per service type, and built to support supervised
delegation to an associate lawyer.

## 2. Nature of Project

Operational architecture and template-development project.
No delivery delegation, no associate hiring, and no live client process changes
are authorized at this stage.

## 3. Strategic Rationale

The Delivery Spine is the structural precondition for:
- credible leverage (LLP-033 associate model requires it)
- quality-consistent delivery at volume
- the 2027 associate hire decision (requires demonstrably repeatable, spec-able,
  reviewable work before hiring is justified)
- eventual firm assetization and the equity sale objective (LLP-030 Priority 6)

Without the Spine:
- hiring will reduce income rather than increase it
- F02 cannot be delegated even partially
- unit economics under leverage cannot be measured
- the Path A vs Path B decision in 2028 will be made blind

## 4. Deliverable (High-Level)

A governed four-stage delivery model (Intake, Diagnosis, Output, Close) with:
- service-specific hour budgets and scope boundaries
- standard templates and checklists per stage
- ML1 QA gate at output
- delegation protocol for associate execution

## 5. Authority

Final approval authority: ML1.
No operational changes to live delivery without explicit ML1 gate approval.

## 6. Dependencies

| Project | Dependency |
|---------|------------|
| LLP-033 Associate Lawyer | Delivery Spine must exist before associate model can be credibly designed |
| LLP-039 Fintech Retainer | Spine governs retainer delivery structure |
| LLP-030 Firm Strategy | Spine is a Priority 6 assetization precondition |
