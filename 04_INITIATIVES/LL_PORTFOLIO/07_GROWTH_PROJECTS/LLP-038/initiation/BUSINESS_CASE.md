# Business Case

The Delivery Spine is the operating structure required before Levine Law can
safely delegate recurring legal work, measure unit economics, or support a
credible associate model. It is a prerequisite for leverage without quality or
capacity collapse.

ML1 approval remains required for scope caps, templates, delegation eligibility,
and stage advancement.
