# Project Charter
Project ID: LLP-033
Project Name: Associate Lawyer Capacity Expansion
Portfolio: 02_Levine_Law_Portfolio
Project Type: Strategic Project
Stage: Initiation

## 1. Purpose
To design and implement an Associate Lawyer role that expands firm legal capacity while preserving quality, supervision standards, and risk controls.

## 2. Nature of Project
Organizational capability expansion.
Role architecture + supervision doctrine.
No hiring authorized until formal approval.

## 3. Strategic Rationale
- Increase revenue capacity.
- Enable leveraged service delivery.
- Support Corporate Clerk oversight.
- Reduce single-point-of-failure risk (ML1 dependency).

## 4. High-Level Deliverable
A formally defined Associate Lawyer framework including:
- Scope of authority
- Supervision model
- Review protocols
- Work allocation system
- Compensation modeling inputs

## 5. Authority
Final approval authority: ML1.

## 6. Promotion Path
Outputs migrate to:
- 03_FIRM_OPERATIONS (role doctrine)
- 04_RISK (supervision controls)
- 02_PRACTICE_AREAS (delegation rules)
- 01_FINANCIAL_MANAGEMENT (cost structure modeling)
