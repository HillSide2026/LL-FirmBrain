# Risk, Cash, and Collections

## Purpose

Hold operating financial-risk, cash, and collections artifacts inside
`01_FINANCIAL_MANAGEMENT`.

## Scope

This folder is for financial-management analysis of:

- insufficient or uneven revenue conversion;
- AR exposure;
- WIP funding burden;
- under-collection;
- liquidity stress;
- cash-risk adjustment;
- collection reliability.

General firm-risk governance remains in `../../04_RISK/` unless the question is
specifically operating financial risk, cash, or collections.
