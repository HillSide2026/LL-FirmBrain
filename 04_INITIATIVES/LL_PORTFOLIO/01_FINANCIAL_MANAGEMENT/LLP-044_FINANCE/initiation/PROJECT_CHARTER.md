# LLP-044 Finance Project Charter

## Purpose

Govern Levine Law finance decisions and finance doctrine inside
`01_FINANCIAL_MANAGEMENT`.

## Scope

In scope:

- external financing and shareholder funding;
- debt facilities, including credit cards and credit lines;
- strategic banking relationships;
- tax-planning interfaces;
- major asset/liability decisions;
- investment and reserve policy;
- corporate finance;
- acquisition financing;
- partner/shareholder distribution structures;
- CFO advisory outputs.

Out of scope:

- bookkeeping;
- management-accounting classification;
- budget-baseline control unless expressly cross-referenced;
- operating cash/collections workflow;
- price-setting, discount approval, or profitability declaration.

## Authority

ML1 is the sole authority for finance decisions and for approving any action
based on finance outputs.
