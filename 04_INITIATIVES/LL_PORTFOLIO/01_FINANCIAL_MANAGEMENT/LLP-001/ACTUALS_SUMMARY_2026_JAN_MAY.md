## Jocelyn Email Summary

Jocelyn's brief review of the January-May 2026 P&L compared to January-May
2025 stated:

- Total revenue increased by `31.6%` (`CAD 61.8k` versus `CAD 47.0k`).
- Direct costs decreased by `24.6%`, mainly because of lower subcontractor and
  legal research expenses.
- Gross profit increased by `48.3%` (`CAD 53.7k` versus `CAD 36.2k`).
- Operating expenses increased by `20.0%`, mainly from higher software
  subscriptions, bank fees, regulatory fees, and rent.
- Marketing expenses remained relatively stable year over year.
- Net profit was `CAD 18.7k` versus `CAD 5.7k` in the prior year, an
  improvement of approximately `230%`.
- Jocelyn described the firm as showing stronger profitability and improved
  operating leverage, while noting the need to continue monitoring software,
  banking, and regulatory expenses.
