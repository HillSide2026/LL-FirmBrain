# Project Plan

**Project:** Sale of Granville (174)
**Project ID:** HBP-013
**Stage:** Planning
**Governing policy:** POL-065 (Matthew Holdings Initiative Risk Policy)

## Planning Objective

Translate the Granville charter into an execution-ready licensing and market-readiness process. Obtain FINTRAC registration, advance RPAA, secure an EMI partner, keep the Granville page/product layer current, and maintain scope discipline.

Product distribution remains a later question. The immediate priority is not a buyer-ready package or a formal regulatory memo; it is securing the EMI path and keeping regulatory status clear enough to make decisions.

## Project Path

1. Obtain FINTRAC registration
2. Prepare and advance RPAA as appropriate
3. Secure an EMI partner or written indication
4. Resolve Kwiikpay — in scope or ruled out
5. Define only the minimum software posture needed to support EMI, regulatory, and operational credibility
6. Keep Granville product/page current
7. Reassess sale packaging only after licensing and EMI posture materially improve
