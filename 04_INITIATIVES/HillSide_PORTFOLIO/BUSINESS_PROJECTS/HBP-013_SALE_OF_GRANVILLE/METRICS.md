## Current Metric Posture

The planning packet is measuring readiness to secure the EMI path and keep the
entity credible while FINTRAC and RPAA move forward.

The key metric is not software breadth.

The key metric is whether 174 has a credible regulatory and EMI path without
depending on an overbuilt support stack.

## Measurement Rule

Planning is complete when the project can answer, with specific evidence:

- is the entity licensed or license-ready?
- is the supporting stack credible but minimal?
- is the EMI path real enough to support the next decision?
- has scope discipline been maintained?
