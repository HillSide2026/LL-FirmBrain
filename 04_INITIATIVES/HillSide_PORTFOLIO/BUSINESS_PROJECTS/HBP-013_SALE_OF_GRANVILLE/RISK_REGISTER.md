# Risk Register

**Project:** Sale of Granville (174)
**Project ID:** HBP-013
**Stage:** Planning
**Governing policy:** POL-065 (Matthew Holdings Initiative Risk Policy)

## Schedule Risks

| ID | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| S-01 | FINTRAC registration is delayed — minimum gate and sale window pushed out | High | High | FINTRAC is top gating metric; escalate timeline uncertainty immediately; no other gate depends on FINTRAC except minimum |
| S-02 | RPAA registration takes longer than expected — preferred gate delayed | Medium | Medium | RPAA advancement is a parallel workstream; document blockers explicitly rather than letting status drift |
| S-03 | Airwallex declines to contract with a pre-revenue entity — EMI milestone slips | Medium | High | Low-commitment approach; secondary EMI outreach authorized from 2026-07-15 if Airwallex path is unclear |
| S-04 | Kwiikpay role remains undefined past 2026-06-30 — preferred gate cannot close | Medium | Medium | Kwiikpay decision is a 2026-06-30 milestone; binary outcome required — in scope or ruled out |
| S-05 | HBP-015 capacity competition delays HBP-013 milestones | High | Medium | Explicit ML1 sequencing decision on HBP-013 vs HBP-015 priority at each milestone |
| S-06 | Rhizome arrangement stalls — compliance software undefined past budget finalization | Medium | Low | Sumsub is the defined fallback; cost differential must be in budget by 2026-07-31 |

## Financial Risks

| ID | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| F-01 | Airwallex contract terms include exclusivity, non-assignment, or punitive exit fees — transaction economics degraded | Medium | High | Transferability is non-negotiable; walk away from terms that cannot provide assignment optionality and change-of-control protection |
| F-02 | Airwallex pre-revenue monthly minimums are higher than expected — carrying cost erodes net proceeds | Medium | Medium | Cost ceiling established at budget finalization 2026-07-31; reject terms above ceiling |
| F-03 | Granville software build costs exceed incremental sale premium | Medium | Medium | Minimum viable posture only; no build scope expansion without ML1 approval |
| F-04 | Distribution investment exceeds the sale premium it creates | Medium | Medium | A credible documented distribution path is the standard — not a live customer base before sale |
| F-05 | Buyer negotiates sale price below working value markers — transaction economics below plan | Medium | Medium | Working value markers are planning assumptions; confirm buyer demand before fixing price expectations |
| F-06 | No buyer found at preferred or stretch gate value — forced to transact at minimum gate floor | Medium | Medium | Minimum gate is always-ready; do not carry preferred/stretch costs indefinitely if buyer demand does not materialize |
