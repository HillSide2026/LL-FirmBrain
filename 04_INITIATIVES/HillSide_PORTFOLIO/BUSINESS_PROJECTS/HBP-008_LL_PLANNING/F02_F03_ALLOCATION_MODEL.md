# F02 / F03 Allocation Model

**Project:** LL Planning
**Project ID:** HBP-008

## Working Hypothesis

For 2026-2027 survival and existential cash-flow protection, F02 Corporate
Health Check is a poor primary investment relative to F03 fintech retainers.

F02 may remain useful as a light referral/intake channel, especially through
accountants and existing trusted relationships, but it should be narrowed to
industries that reinforce LL's strategic lanes. Serious ML1 business-build time
should be allocated first to F03 fintech retainer acquisition and packaging,
subject to the hard scope and capacity controls in LLP-039.

This is a working hypothesis, not a permanent strategy. It must be tested using
the quantitative allocation model below.

## Decision Question

Where should ML1 non-client business-building time be invested over the next
two quarters?

The comparison is:

> Which channel produces more expected controlled cash per ML1 business-build
> hour, while preserving capacity and client quality?

## Channel Roles

| Channel | 2026-2027 role | Build posture |
|---|---|---|
| F02 Corporate Health Check | Light referral/intake channel for CAD 5M+ cash-flow operators in fintech-adjacent, trade-impacted, and Rousseau Mazzuca-referable construction lanes | Maintain, test cheaply, do not treat as primary survival engine |
| F03 fintech retainers | Primary high-cash-flow acquisition hypothesis | Invest serious ML1 build-time, with strict LLP-039 capacity and scope controls |

## Core Formula

```text
Expected controlled value =
(probability of close x expected gross profit)
- acquisition cost
- ML1 non-client build-time cost
- ML1 delivery/supervision burden
- risk adjustment
```

Primary comparison metric:

```text
Expected controlled profit per ML1 business-build hour
```

## F02 Base Case

| Input | Working value |
|---|---:|
| Direct revenue target | CAD 90,000 |
| Paid Health Checks required | 30 |
| Implied average Health Check price | CAD 3,000 |
| Primary risk | Many sales/conversions required for modest direct revenue |
| Strategic upside | Referral trust, downstream remediation, recurring counsel conversion |
| Current posture | Light validation only unless conversion economics improve |

F02 becomes a serious investment only if it proves low-friction paid demand,
strong downstream remediation conversion, recurring counsel conversion, or
accountant/referral leverage that does not consume heavy ML1 time. The preferred
F02 demand should come from the industry lanes defined in
`F02_INDUSTRY_TARGETING_RULE.md`.

## F03 Retainer Base Case

| Retainer mix | Annualized firm collections |
|---|---:|
| 1 x CAD 12,000/month | CAD 144,000 |
| 2 x CAD 12,000/month | CAD 288,000 |
| 3 x CAD 12,000/month | CAD 432,000 |
| 1 x CAD 20,000/month | CAD 240,000 |
| 2 x CAD 20,000/month | CAD 480,000 |

F03 has stronger survival economics because one well-fit CAD 12,000/month
retainer can exceed the entire F02 direct Health Check target on an annualized
basis.

The constraint is not revenue potential. The constraint is scope control and
ML1 capacity.

## F03 Utilization Sensitivity

| Monthly fee | Actual monthly delivery hours | Effective yield |
|---:|---:|---:|
| CAD 12,000 | 15 | CAD 800/hr |
| CAD 12,000 | 20 | CAD 600/hr |

| CAD 20,000 | 25 | CAD 800/hr |
| CAD 20,000 | 33 | ~CAD 606/hr |
| CAD 20,000 | 40 | CAD 500/hr |

The F03 strategy is attractive only if actual utilization stays below the cap
and does not become open-ended fractional legal department access.

## Allocation Rule

Until F02 proves stronger economics, allocate non-client LL build-time as:

| Build-time category | Default allocation |
|---|---:|
| F03 fintech retainer acquisition, packaging, authority, and qualification | 70%-85% |
| F02 industry-specific referral maintenance and low-cost validation | 10%-20% |
| General LL brand/system maintenance | 5%-10% |

F02 may not receive more than 20% of non-client LL build-time unless ML1 records
one of the upgrade triggers below.

Below-CAD-5M cash-flow prospects should be routed away from the Health Check
service path and toward monetizable information products where possible.

## F02 Upgrade Triggers

Upgrade F02 from light/referral posture to serious investment only if at least
two of the following are true in a rolling 90-day period:

| Trigger | Threshold |
|---|---:|
| Paid Health Checks | 4+ completed |
| Direct Health Check revenue | CAD 12,000+ collected |
| Downstream conversion | 2+ paid remediation or recurring counsel engagements |
| Referral leverage | 2+ accountant/referral sources produce qualified leads |
| ML1 burden | <=2 ML1 non-client build hours per paid Health Check |
| Effective downstream value | CAD 10,000+ average downstream value per converted Health Check |

If these triggers are not met, F02 remains a lightweight channel.

## F03 Stop / Slow Triggers

Slow or pause F03 retainer acquisition if any of the following occur:

| Trigger | Required action |
|---|---|
| More than 3 concurrent fintech retainers | Stop selling; reset capacity |
| Any retainer becomes open-ended legal department access | Stop selling; repair scope |
| CAD 12,000 retainer exceeds 25 delivery hours/month for 2 consecutive months | Reprice, narrow, or offboard |
| CAD 20,000 retainer exceeds 40 delivery hours/month for 2 consecutive months | Reprice, narrow, or offboard |
| 2026 matter requires premium adjacent scope | Defer, refer, waitlist, or separate-scope only by ML1 exception |

## Modeling Cases

| Case | Planning implication | Current recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| F02 Primary | Requires 30 Health Checks / CAD 90,000 direct revenue; high number of conversions for modest direct cash | Do not treat as primary without upgrade triggers |
| F03 Primary | Requires 1-2 well-fit recurring mandates; main risk is scope creep and overload | Primary build-time allocation |
| Hybrid | F03 receives serious build-time; F02 remains light referral infrastructure | Default 2026-2027 posture |

## Decision Standard

The default 2026-2027 allocation is **Hybrid with F03 priority**.

Move to F03-only if F02 consumes attention without paid demand.

Move back toward F02 only if the F02 upgrade triggers prove that Health Checks
create low-burden downstream value.
