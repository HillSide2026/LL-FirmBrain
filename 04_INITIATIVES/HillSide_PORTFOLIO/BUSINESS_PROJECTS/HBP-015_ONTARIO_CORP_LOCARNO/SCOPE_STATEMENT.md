# Scope Statement

**Project:** Ontario Corp / Locarno
**Project ID:** HBP-015
**Stage:** Planning

## In Scope

This planning stage covers:

- verifying and documenting FINTRAC standing for `1001494374 Ontario Corp`
- documenting RPAA registration status and share-transfer implications
- building and documenting the AML program to dormant / pre-operational standard
- cleaning and confirming corporate records for Track A readiness
- pursuing direct EMI relationship (ConnectPay primary target) for Track B
- defining minimum software posture (Rhizome default unless EMI requires Sumsub)
- assessing Track C viability — direct sponsor-bank relationship
- assembling a market-ready diligence package at the achieved track level
- working through the product distribution question — identifying channels, acquisition model, and first customer path for Locarno
- selecting the sale track (A, B, or C) based on institutional build progress
- preparing for ML1-approved sale outreach at the right time

All three tracks remain open throughout planning.

## Out of Scope

This planning stage does not authorize:

- running meaningful funds-flow volume through the entity
- building a live operating payments business
- marketing a BaaS, neobank, or standalone software product under Locarno
- active sale outreach, LOI negotiation, or transaction closing
- migrating FinSure, Levine Law, or other operating lines into this entity

## Scope Boundary

The asset being governed is `1001494374 Ontario Corp` — the regulated entity itself. The Locarno product is the value-add layer that makes the entity more attractive to a Track B or C buyer. Software and institutional relationships matter only insofar as they increase buyer confidence and sale value.

## Track Scope

| Track | In Scope This Stage |
|---|---|
| Track A | FINTRAC verification, AML program, clean corporate records, buyer handoff memo |
| Track B | All Track A work, plus EMI relationship (ConnectPay), RPAA readiness, ledger and AML software, Locarno product layer |
| Track C | All Track B work, plus direct sponsor-bank relationship assessment and pursuit |
