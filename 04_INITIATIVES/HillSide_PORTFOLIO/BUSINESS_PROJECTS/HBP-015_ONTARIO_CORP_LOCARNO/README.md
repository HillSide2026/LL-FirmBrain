# Ontario Corp / Locarno

**Project ID:** `HBP-015`
**Supersedes:** HBP-005 (retired 2026-05-31)

This project covers `1001494374 Ontario Corp` and the Locarno product across all three tracks.

## Entity and Product — Distinction

**`1001494374 Ontario Corp`**
The regulated entity. An Ontario-incorporated company within the Matthew Holdings structure. FINTRAC MSB registered. RPAA registration held. A registration-first payment operating layer with direct regulated financial institution connectivity.

**Locarno**
The product within the entity. An international payments and treasury platform for service businesses with multi-currency and cross-border needs. See `LOCARNO_PRODUCT.md` for the full product description.

## Three Tracks

**Track A — Pure MSB Shell Sale**
Sell `1001494374 Ontario Corp` as a clean MSB shell. FINTRAC active, AML program documented, corporate records clean, no legacy liabilities. Working value marker: CAD $50,000. The Ontario corporation advantage: no Canadian director requirement, more portable for certain buyer profiles.

**Track B — Infrastructure Asset**
Build the entity into a regulated payments infrastructure asset with direct EMI relationship (ConnectPay as primary target) and RPAA-operational controls. Three sub-gates: Minimum ($50k), Preferred ($200k), Stretch ($650k).

**Track C — Direct Sponsor-Bank**
Track B plus a direct sponsor-bank relationship (contractual, referenceable). Adds a second institutional layer. Working value marker: TBD above Track B Stretch.

## Objective

Build `1001494374 Ontario Corp` into a licensed, market-ready regulated payments asset and eventually sell it on an ML1-approved timetable, with proceeds flowing into `17513721 Canada Inc`.
