# Trade Remedies and Market Access — Practice Area

## Scope

This practice area covers LL matters involving:

- Trade remedies: anti-dumping, countervailing duties, safeguards
- Market access strategy: tariff navigation, origin analysis, non-tariff barriers
- Regulatory and tariff navigation for Canadian exporters and importers

The current primary sector focus is Canadian wood products. The framework is
sector-specific where noted and general where not.
