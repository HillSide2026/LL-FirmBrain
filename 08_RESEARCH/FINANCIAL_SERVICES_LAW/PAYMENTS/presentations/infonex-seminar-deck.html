Shrinking Mitigation Windows

Liquidity Risks in a Real-Time Environment

Liquidity Risk

Intraday liquidity must be available at all times. There is no overnight settlement window to net positions before the next business day.

Operational Consequence

Institutions require real-time liquidity monitoring and pre-funded liquidity buffers. A failed replenishment event can immediately disrupt outgoing payment activity.

Fraud Management in a Real-Time Environment

Compressed Decision Windows

Fraud decisions must often be made in seconds rather than hours or days.

Greater Reliance on Automation

Real-time payments require automated risk scoring, behavioural analytics, and authentication controls that operate at transaction speed.

Reduced Recovery Opportunities

Once funds have settled, opportunities to stop or recover fraudulent transfers may be significantly reduced.

Key Takeaway

Real-time settlement leaves little opportunity to detect and stop fraud after a payment is initiated. Fraud controls must increasingly operate before or during authorization.
